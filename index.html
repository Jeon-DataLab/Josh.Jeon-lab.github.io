Career

Pacific Northwest National Laboratory &
Northeastern University

Data Scientist Intern July 2023 - September 2023

My internship with the partnership of Pacific Northwest National Laboratory and Northeastern was a deep dive into the world of Urban Resiliency. The team and I were given the challenge of creating a system to autonomously process and visualize resilience metrics. We began with a broad scope and limited direction, tasked with determining, gathering of data sources and the product's design. I took the initiative to scrape data from the 100 Resilient Cities reports, identfying and analyzing 1,040 key terms using NLP, and then crafting a dataset that we normalized in percentile and presented through a Tableau dashboard. We also developed a chatbot powered by RoBerTa and Haystack, capable of answering questions about urban resilience. Project culminated in a normination to present the work to the Northeastern University President, a significant honor. The internship had challenges, with constant team changes and steep learning curves, such as creating a Chat Bot and applying NLP. However, with proactive 'I do' approach, I dedicated to successfully ensure the project success which was rewarding.
